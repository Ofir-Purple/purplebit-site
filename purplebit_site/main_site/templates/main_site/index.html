Hello, We're Purple Bit

We create beautiful and usable web applications for our customers using a Lean Development Process, built on Django and AngularJS.

Purple Bit was founded by veterans of an elite programming unit in the Israeli Army , with over 10 years of experience building products and managing software teams. We built Purple Bit with two goals: create amazing products through technical excellence, and build a team of super-smart developers who love programming.

We've worked with everything from small 1-man companies to the largest organisations, with customers from around the world. We're incredibly proud of the products we've built, and we've had the great fortune to work with many amazing clients and launch amazing products.

Our process is built on a Lean/Agile methodology, which we have been using and teaching for many years.

Training
